SesS - Smart Stock Detection Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2025
Effective Date: June 23, 2025
Quick Summary: SesS detects companies on websites you visit and shows stock information. We only collect minimal data needed for this service and don't store personal information.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Website URLs: Current page URLs to detect company mentions
  • Company Detection Data: When we identify a publicly traded company on a webpage
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about extension usage to improve performance

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Complete browsing history
  • Login credentials or passwords
  • Financial account details
  • Keystroke data or form inputs
  • Personal messages or communications

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Detect publicly traded companies mentioned on webpages
  • Fetch and display real-time stock prices and market data
  • Provide direct links to trading platforms
  • Improve the accuracy of company detection algorithms
  • Monitor extension performance and fix technical issues

Third-Party Services

Financial Data Providers

We use financial APIs (such as Polygon.io, Alpha Vantage, or Yahoo Finance) to fetch stock prices. Only stock ticker symbols are shared - no personal data.

Trading Platform Links

When you click broker links, you're redirected to external websites. We don't share your data with these platforms automatically - each has their own privacy policies.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics to improve the extension. This includes company detection events and user interaction patterns, but no personal identifying information.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: Extension settings stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't store your personal data on our servers
  • Secure Connections: All API requests use HTTPS encryption
  • Automatic Expiration: Cached data expires automatically (stock prices after 5 minutes, company detection after 24 hours)

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Disable: Turn off the extension anytime in Chrome settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension and all local data
  • Control Permissions: Manage what sites the extension can access
  • Data Deletion: Contact us to request deletion of any analytics data

Data Retention

  • Session Data: Cleared when browser closes
  • Stock Cache: Expires after 5 minutes
  • Company Detection: Expires after 24 hours
  • Analytics: Retained by Google Analytics for 26 months (anonymous data only)

Children's Privacy

SesS is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

International Users

This extension complies with privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA. Users in different jurisdictions may have additional rights under local privacy laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Transparency

All data collection is transparent and serves the direct purpose of providing stock information overlays. We do not engage in:

  • Data selling or sharing for profit
  • Cross-platform tracking or profiling
  • Behavioral advertising
  • Unnecessary data retention
